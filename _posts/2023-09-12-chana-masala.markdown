This Chana Masala recipe is a hearty delicious meal but easy enough to throw together on a weekday night.

Ingredients
- 2 cans chickpeas
- 2 cans petite diced tomatoes
- 4 cloves garlic
- 1 small onion
- 1 tsp salt

Directions
- Put it all together in a pot and cook it
